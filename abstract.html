Call for Abstracts

Abstract Submission

Participants who wish to present an oral or poster presentation should submit an abstract by the deadline.

Submission Guidelines

  • Presentation types: Oral presentation or poster presentation
  • Abstract length: maximum 300 words
  • Keywords: 3–5 keywords
  • Language: English
  • Submission deadline: March 31, 2027

Required Information

  1. Presentation title
  2. Author names and affiliations
  3. Presenting author contact information
  4. Preferred presentation type
  5. Abstract text
