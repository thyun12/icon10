About

About the Conference

ICoN10 will bring together researchers, students, and professionals to discuss recent advances in microbial ecology, environmental biotechnology, and sequencing-based approaches for understanding complex microbial systems.

The meeting will include invited lectures, contributed oral presentations, poster sessions, networking events, and opportunities for early-career researchers to present their work.
